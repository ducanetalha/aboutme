Family

Ducane comes from a hardworking and close-knit family. He has two older brothers, both of whom are engineers. His father is a veterinarian and also helps Ducane’s mother with their tomato farm. His mother is an excellent cook and loves to share her recipes with family and friends. She’s also a natural teacher and has been a strong inspiration for Ducane’s own love of teaching. Throughout his high school years, Ducane studied in three different cities to explore new regions of Turkey and meet new people. This journey helped him build lifelong friendships and taught him to adapt to different environments. You can see a short video of Ducane and his friends below.
